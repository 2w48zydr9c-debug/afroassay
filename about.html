About Me

Your independent mineral processing consultant

👤

Independent Mineral Processing Consultant

I am an independent mineral processing consultant specializing in tantalum, niobium, tungsten, tin, and molybdenum. From sampling to gravimetric analysis, I master every step of the assay process myself.

I have participated in the establishment of six geological exploration institutions and have conducted multiple on-site inspections of African mines, deeply understanding the realities and challenges of small-scale mining across the continent.

My mission is simple: give African miners accurate data about their ore and a direct channel to Chinese buyers — so low-grade ore doesn't go to waste and miners get paid what they deserve.
